Jika anda lagi mencari jawaban mengenai pertanyaan jadikan kalimat berikut ini menjadi kalimat passive dengan menggunakan b.inggris :
1. I agree with dr. ikeda's theory
2. Dr. ikeda developed that theory
3. Timmy dropped the cup
4. The assistant manager iterviewed me
5. A hurricane destroyed the small fishing village
6. After class, one of the students always erases the chalk board
7. Someone made this antique table in 1734
8. Someone stole my purse
9. Jim's daughter drew that picture. My son drew this picture
10. Teachers teach reading in the first grade ente berada di website yang tepat. Kami mempunyai 1 jawaban atas jadikan kalimat berikut ini menjadi kalimat passive dengan menggunakan b.inggris :
1. I agree with dr. ikeda's theory
2. Dr. ikeda developed that theory
3. Timmy dropped the cup
4. The assistant manager iterviewed me
5. A hurricane destroyed the small fishing village
6. After class, one of the students always erases the chalk board
7. Someone made this antique table in 1734
8. Someone stole my purse
9. Jim's daughter drew that picture. My son drew this picture
10. Teachers teach reading in the first grade. Silakan pelajari jawaban selengkapnya di bawah:

Jawaban: #1:

1. Dr. Ikeda's theory is agreed with by me.

2. That theory was developed by Dr. Ikeda.

3. The cup was dropped by Timmy.

4. I was interviewed by the assistant manager.

5. The small fishing village was destroyed by a hurricane.

6. After class, the chalk board is always erased by one of the students.

7. This antique table was made by someone in 1734.

8. My purse was stolen by someone.

9. That picture was drawn by Jim's daughter and this picture was drawn by my son.

10. Reading was taught by teachers in the first grade.

Pembahasan

1. Sebenarnya kalimat ini tidak tepat bila diubah ke dalam passive, alasannya karena passive terjadi dalam kata kerja transitive yaitu kata kerja yang memerlukan benda. Tapi bila harus diubah ke dalam passive maka formatnya menjadi seperti di atas (dengan menyertakan with).

2. Kalimat dalam past tense maka menjadi "was developed."

3. Kalimat past tense menjadi "was dropped by."

4. Kalimat past tense menjadi "was interviewed by."

5. Kalimat past tense menjadi "was destroyed by."

6. Kalimat present tense menjadi "is always erased."

7. Kalimat past tense menjadi "was made by."

8. Kalimat past tense menjadi "was stolen by."

9. Kalimat past tense menjadi "was drawn by."

10. Kalimat past tense menjadi "was taught by."
