Daughter : Yes, I went to see her yesterday.

Father : Have you seen Grandpa lately?

Daughter : No, I haven’t seen him. When I was visiting Grandma, ..... .He had gone out

shopping for groceries.

A. he isn’t there.

B. he hasn’t been there.

C. he wasn’t there.

D. he won’t be there.

E. he hadn’t been there.
